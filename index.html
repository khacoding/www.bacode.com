Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how I, as an independent freelancer, collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit my website or engage my services. Please read this policy carefully to understand my practices regarding your personal data.

Last Updated: June 15, 2025

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Information I Collect

Personal Information

I may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to me when you:

  • Contact me through forms or email
  • Subscribe to my newsletter
  • Engage my services as a client
  • Make payments through Stripe

Payment Information

When you make payments through Stripe, I do not store your payment details. Stripe securely processes all payments and I only receive:

  • Transaction ID
  • Payment amount
  • Payment status
  • Your name and email associated with the payment

How I Use Your Information

I use the information I collect for various purposes, including:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain my services
  • To communicate with you regarding your projects
  • To process payments and send payment confirmations
  • To improve my website and services

Stripe Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely through Stripe. I never have access to your credit card details. Stripe adheres to the highest security standards including PCI-DSS compliance. For more information, please see Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

I respect your privacy and do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, I may share information in the following situations:

  • Payment Processing: With Stripe to process payments
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law
